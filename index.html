Our Journey

Dr. Graham's Homes, established in 1900 by Reverend Dr. John Anderson Graham, has been a beacon of hope and education for children from diverse backgrounds. Our mission remains unchanged: to provide holistic education and care.

Historical Milestones

1900: Foundation

Reverend Dr. John Anderson Graham establishes St. Andrew's Colonial Homes

1925: Silver Jubilee

Celebrating 25 years of transformative education

1947: Renaming

Officially becomes Dr. Graham's Homes

2025: 125th Anniversary

Continuing the legacy of care and education
